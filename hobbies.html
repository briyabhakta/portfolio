I love listening to music. My favorite band is Coldplay. I saw them in concert when I was in high school, and I had a great time. Some of my other favorite artists are Wallows, Ed Sheeran, and Brockhampton. My music taste varies, and has evolved immensely over the years. I listen to many different genres; what I listen to each day usually reflects my mood. I have been to many concerts, including OneRepublic, Big Sean, Coldplay, Wallows, Ed Sheeran, and Shawn Mendes. I had a great time at all these concerts- Coldplay and Wallows were my favorite ones. Another one of my favorite albums is the Hamilton soundtrack. I saw the musical in Chicago in October 2018, and I loved it.

I also love to bake. Especially when I am back home in Canton, I bake all the time- cakes, cookies, and pies. I recently baked a cheesecake that was amazing. My dad loves coconut, so I usually make him coconut cream pie on his birthdays. In my free time, I like to bake, do puzzles, and hang out with friends.
